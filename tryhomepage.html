WHAT IS THE LGBTQIA+ COMMUNITY?

The LGBT community is a loosely defined grouping of lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, and other queer individuals united by a common culture and social movements. These communities generally celebrate pride, diversity, individuality, and sexuality.

OpenSpace is a website where anyone part of the LGBTQIA+ community and an ally to the community, may write, read, and discuss their favorite literature. Try and visit us to learn more.
